FAQs


What is Share AdVantage?

Think of us as your new eco-friendly BFF! We give out stylish paper bags to retailers for free, and they sport ads from cool businesses like yours. It’s a win-win for the planet and your brand!


How does Share AdVantage work?

We hand out our branded chic, eco-friendly bags to retailers, who then carry your awesome ads showcasing your brand in every hand. No strings attached—except for the ones on the bags, of course!


What’s the story behind the name Share AdVantage?

Glad you asked! We picked “Share” because we love spreading good vibes and eco-friendly paper bags totally free of charge to retailers for eliminating plastic usage. “AdVantage” is our nod to the awesome perks your brand gets with our creative ad approach. So, put them together, and you’ve got a name that shows how we’re all about sharing the benefits of smart, sustainable advertising while giving a high-five to the planet. It’s our way of making advertising feel good and do good!

Imagine your brand getting a gold star for being green while also snagging the spotlight! Our eco-friendly bags make your brand look good and do good. It’s like winning a trophy for being awesome and planet-friendly.


How does advertising on paper bags benefit my business?

Think of it as giving your brand a VIP pass to the coolest party in town. Your ad gets featured on stylish, sustainable bags, making you a hero in the world of marketing and green living. Talk about a win-win!
