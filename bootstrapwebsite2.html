Rising Tide of Plastic: India’s Urgent Need for Sustainable Solutions

India is facing a severe plastic waste crisis, generating approximately 26,000 tons of plastic waste daily—equivalent to about 9.46 million tons annually. A staggering 40% of this waste comes from single-use plastic bags, which pose a significant threat to our environment and public health.

only about 60% is recycled. A whopping 70% of India's plastic waste is generated by the Fast-Moving Consumer Goods (FMCG) sector, further intensifying the problem.

rivers like the Ganga and Yamuna are increasingly polluted, threatening marine life and human health. .

This crisis isn’t just a statistic; it’s a growing threat to our environment and future generations. Now is the time to act, for the sake of our planet and the legacy we leave behind.

Collaborative Advertisement.

Collaborative Marketing leverages the power of partnership to amplify advertising efforts by combining multiple brands’ messages on a single platform. In our innovative approach, we print up to three distinct advertisements on each paper bag, allowing brands to share both the cost and the spotlight. This strategy not only reduces individual advertising expenses but also maximizes visibility, as consumers engage with a diverse array of offers and products all in one place.

Our Social Mission

At Share AdVantage, We are dedicated to eliminating plastic bag use and replacing them with eco-friendly, reusable paper bags that not only reduce waste but also spread awareness through impactful branding. Every bag we produce is a step towards a cleaner, greener India. We believe that businesses and consumers have a shared responsibility to protect our environment.

Our goal is not just to reduce plastic waste but to inspire a nationwide shift towards sustainability. By partnering with us, brands contribute to a mission that saves our wildlife, cleans our water, and reduces the 26,000 tons of plastic waste India produces daily. Together, we can break the cycle of pollution and create a legacy of positive impact for generations to come.
